Radhakishan Damani

Indian businessman, investor & founder of DMart

Born 12 July 1945
Known For DMart, Value Investing
Traits Simplicity, Discipline, Patience

Radhakishan Shivkishan Damani is an Indian billionaire businessman and one of the country’s most respected value investors. He gained early recognition in the stock market for his disciplined, long-term approach and deep focus on fundamentals. Later, he founded DMart, a supermarket chain built on the principles of everyday low pricing, cost efficiency and customer trust. Despite his success, he is known for his simplicity, humility and preference for staying away from the limelight.
